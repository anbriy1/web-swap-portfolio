Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NAME-PROJECT

  • What is NAME-PROJECT?
    NAME-PROJECT is a decentralized exchange (DEX) built on Tempo network, enabling fast and secure token swaps with minimal fees.
    Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with user-friendly design to deliver
    the ultimate trading experience.
  • How do I swap tokens on NAME-PROJECT?
    Simply connect your wallet, select the tokens you want to trade, choose your amount, and confirm the transaction.
    Our smart routing algorithm automatically finds the best rates and liquidity pools
    for your swap.
  • What is the STORM token?
    STORM is the governance token of NAME-PROJECT, launching on Tempo network. Token holders can vote on platform decisions,
    earn trading fee discounts, stake for yield rewards,
    and participate in exclusive ecosystem benefits.
  • Why choose Tempo network?
    Tempo network offers blazing-fast transaction speeds, minimal transaction costs, and high security standards.
    Building NAME-PROJECT on Tempo ensures our users enjoy
    the best possible trading experience.
  • Is my money safe on NAME-PROJECT?
    Yes! NAME-PROJECT employs non-custodial swaps, meaning you maintain full control of your assets at all times.
    Our smart contracts have undergone comprehensive security audits
    and follow industry best practices.
